Features
Everything Spotify
should have built in.
💊
Dynamic Island
A morphing pill at the top of your screen. Collapse it to a minimal indicator, expand it to see full controls and navigation — all in one fluid interface.
💊
🎤
Synced Lyrics
Word-by-word synced lyrics from lrclib.net. Full-screen lyrics mode with album art. Toggle on/off per song.
🌊
musican® Visualizer
Frequency-reactive bars that move with the beat. Appears automatically after inactivity. Two modes: visualizer or full-screen lyrics.
🤖
AI DJ
Spotify's AI-powered DJ builds a personalized mix based on your listening history, taste, and mood. Always fresh.
🎨
Fully Customizable
Accent colors, 3 home layouts, player bar styles, toggle every element. The background follows your album art.
🎛️
7-Band Equalizer
Browser-based EQ with presets (Bass Boost, Pop, Rock, Jazz) and fully adjustable custom bands.
📚
Full Library
Your playlists, albums, and followed artists. Create playlists, add tracks, follow/unfollow artists — everything.
⬆
Auto-Updates
When a new version is available, a notification appears. One click to download and install — no manual steps.
Setup

3
Connect

4
Enjoy
Log in once. myspotify starts automatically with Windows.
